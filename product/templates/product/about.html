Introduction

Shamridhi Vikash Sales Cooperative (formely SVSC) is established under Government ruls and its Head Office is at Punjab.

SVSC is a highly potential, transparent, small invesment, fully cash back and free gift with bonus plan, that "goal in only two steps ahead".

The company main motive is not to create group only but empowers and upgrade the status of middle class to have good for which they can't afford.

Choose your group gift from 'SVSC' product group list

How to get your pin ?

It is mandatory for every member to register with SVSC and got your pin through sponser having unique SVSC pin

After selection of group of 'Gift Item', It is mandatory for every member to register by Form 'B' and got your pin through sponser having unique SVSC PIN by your cooperation of gift "selected group only one".

On registration and getting PIN as a co-seller you become sponser for your chain

How to Complete your chain?

The sponser have to introduce 4 seller with pin and your all 4 seller have to introduces 4 co-sellers each with pin "having their own passwords" Your chain is complete.

This chain is also forwarded and got each PIN holder seperately selected gift on chain completion

Cash back of your cooperation distribution

The cooperation of all co-sellers distributed as follows:

Co-operation --> sponser --> seller --> company

Claim of your Gift

In completion of your chain you have to send a detail report of your chain by filling the given Form C . After detail reporting and and its verification, you messaged and receive your gift from SVSC store

Non-Active PIN Holder

  • If any co-seller do not active and want to transfer his/her PIN to another, He/She have to be charged of Rs 500 and re-issued PIN in new given person as new co-seller.
  • If any co-seller do not want to act he/she has only margin money refunded back and his/her pin will be locked. The company is not responsible for any loss and no claim for gift
  • A PIN is active for only 3 months/ 90 days from date of issue. After 90 days he/she has to pay Rs 300 to extend for next 90 days validity
  • On failing of validity, only margin money would be refunded to PIN holder
