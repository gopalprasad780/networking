Introduction

Shamridhi Vikash Sales Cooperative (formely SVSC) is established under Government rules and its Head Office is at Chandigarh,Punjab.

SVSC is a highly potential, transparent, small invesment, fully cash back and free gift with bonus plan, that "goal in only two steps ahead".

The company main motive is not to create group only but empowers and upgrade the status of middle class to have good for which they can't afford.

Choose your group of gift from 'SVSC' product group list A

It is mandatory to every member to register by Form 'B' and got your pin through sponser having unique SVSC PIN on your cooperation money for your selected gift.

On registration and getting PIN as a co-seller you become sponser for your chain

How to Complete your chain?

The sponser have to introduce 4 seller with pin and your all 4 seller also have to introduce 4 co-sellers each with pin. Your chain is complete. Simple !!

(All pin holder have unique PIN and own password )

This chain is also forwarded and each PIN holder got seperately selected gift on chain completion

Cash back of your cooperation distribution

The cooperation of all co-sellers distributed as follows:

Co-operation --> sponser --> seller --> company

Claim of your Gift

In completion of your chain you have to send a detailed reports of your chain by filling the given Form C . After detail reporting and its verification, you be messaged and received your gift from SVSC nearest branch store.

Non-Active PIN Holder

  • If any co-seller not active and want to transfer his/her PIN to another, would be charged of Rs 500 and re-issued PIN in new given person as a new co-seller on request by proper way
  • If any co-seller not active, On surrender of PIN, his/her PIN would be locked and only consolation money would be refunded to his/her bank account. Further, the company is not responsible for any loss and claim for gift
  • Any PIN is active for only 3 months/ 90 days from date of issue. After 90 days he/she has to pay Rs 500 to extend for next 90 days validity
  • On failing of validity, only consolation money would be refunded to PIN holder's bank account.

Note:

  • There is no agent or employee in SVSC. All members are seller or co-seller based on 'Sabka Sath Sabka Vikash'.
  • It is restricted or your responsibility to share your SVSC PIN password to other for any loss.
  • Any person may join SVSC with sound knowledge and above 18 years old in one or more time with same or other group of plan
  • Any member could not interchange his/her gift selection from another group of gifts.
  • Any new member is free to choose his/her any gift from the sponser group of gifts.
  • A sponser introduce only those seller who also like or choose the same gift group from given product list group A
  • The grade of Sponser- Seller - Co-Seller is upgraded gradually as
    • Co-seller got pin > sponser
    • Sponser introduce 4 new member-pin > seller
    • seller introduce 4 new member-pin > co-seller
  • No one member be thritened or suppressed to join SVSC and product group of gift-selection
  • Without completion of chain there is no claim of gift.
